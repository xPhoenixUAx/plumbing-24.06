Information Use

How service request information is handled.

This policy explains how FlowCore Plumbing handles information submitted through this website, by phone, or by email. The website is designed for service inquiries, estimate requests, maintenance questions, and customer communication.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, service address, preferred appointment timing, and details about the plumbing issue you describe. If you send photos or notes, we use them only to understand the requested service.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to plumbing service requests and estimate questions.
  • To route requests and help confirm follow-up details.
  • To share relevant request details with matching or communication tools.
  • To maintain internal records related to submitted requests.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with communication tools or independent service providers only when needed to respond to your request or maintain business records.

Contact

Questions about privacy may be sent to service@flowcoreplumbing.com.
