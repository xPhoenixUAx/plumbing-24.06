How service request information is collected, used, shared, and protected.

This Privacy Policy explains how FlowCore Plumbing collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use this website, submit a request, call, email, search for services, or interact with our provider matching functionality.

FlowCore Plumbing operates as a service inquiry and referral platform. We do not directly perform plumbing work. Information you provide may be used to help route your inquiry to independent local providers, communication tools, or support systems that help respond to your request.

Information You Provide

We may collect information that you choose to submit through forms, phone calls, email, text messages, or other website interactions.

  • Name and contact information, including phone number and email address.
  • Service address, city, ZIP code, or general location details.
  • Service category, urgency, preferred timing, and request details.
  • Descriptions of leaks, drain issues, water heater concerns, sewer symptoms, fixture needs, or commercial plumbing requests.
  • Photos, notes, access instructions, property details, or other information you choose to provide.
  • Communications with us, including call, email, SMS, or form submission records.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the website, certain technical information may be collected automatically to support performance, security, analytics, and basic website operations.

  • IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and approximate location derived from technical data.
  • Pages viewed, links clicked, referral source, session activity, and general interaction data.
  • Cookie identifiers, analytics identifiers, and similar technologies where enabled.
  • Date, time, and diagnostic information related to website usage or errors.

How We Use Information

We use information for practical website and service-request purposes, including:

  • Responding to inquiries and service request submissions.
  • Helping connect users with independent providers who may respond to the request.
  • Organizing request details so providers can understand the issue, urgency, and property context.
  • Contacting users by phone, email, SMS, or other communication channels about submitted inquiries.
  • Improving website usability, forms, routing, content, and service category guidance.
  • Monitoring website performance, preventing misuse, and maintaining security.
  • Keeping internal records for business, support, quality, compliance, and legal purposes.

Provider Matching and Information Sharing

Because this website is designed to help users connect with independent providers, request information may be shared with third parties when needed to respond to, route, or support your inquiry.

  • Independent local providers who may contact you about your request.
  • Call centers, dispatch, CRM, email, SMS, form processing, or communication platforms.
  • Analytics, hosting, security, advertising, and website operation vendors.
  • Professional advisors, legal authorities, or other parties when required by law or necessary to protect rights, safety, or security.

Independent providers are separate businesses. Their privacy practices, service terms, pricing, licensing, insurance, communications, and work quality are their own responsibility.

Communications Consent

By submitting a request, you authorize FlowCore Plumbing, our communication tools, and independent providers to contact you about your inquiry using the contact information you provide. Communications may include calls, emails, SMS messages, or other follow-up related to your request. Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a guarantee of service availability.

Cookies, Analytics, and Advertising

We may use cookies, pixels, tags, analytics tools, advertising tools, and similar technologies to understand website usage, improve performance, remember preferences, measure marketing, and support approved campaigns. More detail is available in our Cookie Policy.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for business, operational, communication, recordkeeping, legal, security, and compliance purposes. Retention periods may vary based on the type of information, the nature of the request, provider follow-up, applicable laws, and internal business needs.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. However, no website, internet transmission, database, or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You should avoid submitting sensitive information that is not needed for a service inquiry.

User Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, or limitation of certain personal information. You may also ask questions about information we maintain or request that we stop contacting you for non-essential communications where applicable.

To make a request, contact us using the information below. We may need to verify your identity before responding. Some information may be retained when required for legal, security, transactional, or legitimate business reasons.

Do Not Sell or Share

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary sense of exchanging it directly for money. Because this website may operate as a referral or lead generation service, submitted request details may be shared with independent providers to respond to your inquiry. Some privacy laws may define certain advertising, analytics, or referral activities as "sharing" or "selling." You may contact us with questions or applicable opt-out requests.

Third-Party Websites and Services

This website may link to third-party websites or use third-party platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, security, content, or policies of third-party websites, providers, platforms, or services.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Service Area and Jurisdiction

This website is intended for service inquiries in the service area described on the site, including Greater Chicago and nearby suburbs. Privacy rights and obligations may vary based on your location and applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with the effective date shown above. Continued use of the website after changes are posted means the updated policy applies to future interactions.
