Questions Before You Request

Clear answers for homeowners using a provider matching service.

These answers explain how the connection works, what remains your choice, and what to confirm before approving plumbing work.

Does FlowCore Plumbing perform the plumbing work?

No. FlowCore Plumbing is a free matching service. We help organize your request and connect you with independent local providers who may be able to follow up.

Do I have to hire the first provider who contacts me?

No. You choose whether to hire any provider. Review the provider details, scope, estimate, license, insurance, and timing before approving work.

Are the starting ranges guaranteed prices?

No. Starting ranges are general examples. Final pricing depends on access, timing, parts, property conditions, and the final scope you approve with the provider.

What details should I share in a request?

Share the affected fixture or area, visible symptoms, when the issue started, whether water is actively leaking or backing up, and the best way to reach you.

Can I request urgent plumbing help?

Yes. Use the priority line for active leaks, backups, no hot water, sewer odors, or fixture failures. Provider availability can vary by location and schedule.

Will my request details be shared?

Request details may be shared with independent providers or communication tools when needed to respond to your inquiry. Review the privacy policy for more information.
