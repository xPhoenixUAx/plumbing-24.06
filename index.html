Our Process

We keep the matching process clear, quick, and easy to understand.

Every request follows a clear sequence so homeowners and property managers can share useful details before speaking with a local provider.

STEP 1

Share The Issue

You tell us the affected area, visible symptoms, timing, and urgency so the request can be routed clearly.

STEP 2

Connect & Discuss

An independent provider may contact you to discuss availability, inspection needs, pricing, and next steps.

STEP 3

Verify & Decide

You choose whether to hire the provider and remain responsible for verifying license, insurance, and fit.
